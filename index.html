CRASHBOUND — battle prototype

Phase 1 · proving the launch→detonate combo. Faster fighters act sooner on the timeline up top.

How the combo works

You control Brand (front-line bruiser) and Wisp (back-line mage). Each fights when their turn comes up on the timeline.

The hook: set up, then cash in.
• Brand → Uppercut launches an enemy into the air (✦ airborne).
• Wisp → Frostbite freezes an enemy (❄ frozen).
• Wisp → Meteor does huge bonus damage to an airborne or frozen target — the DETONATE.

Launching/freezing also shoves the enemy back on the timeline, so they lose their turn. Trade that lock for damage by detonating.

Also: Rend bleeds, Meteor burns (damage over time), and Gust yanks the back enemy into melee. Survive 3 encounters — patrol, an elite, then the Warlord.
